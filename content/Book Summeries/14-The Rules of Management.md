- Treat people with respect and clarity: communicate expectations, listen, and give feedback in a firm, fair way.
    
- Set clear goals and roles so everyone knows what they are responsible for and how success is measured.
    
- Lead by example; your behavior often matters more than your words in shaping team culture.
    
- Delegate effectively: give tasks _with_ ownership, not just orders, and support, but don’t micromanage.
    
- Manage yourself first (time, stress, priorities), because a disorganized manager creates a disorganized team.
