- Capture _everything_ in a trusted system (inbox, notes, or task app) so nothing floats in your head.
    
- Clarify each item: decide the next physical action (e.g., “email X”, “book meeting”)—no vague tasks like “project”.
    
- Organize tasks into categories (contexts, projects, due dates) so you can pick the right one at the right time.
    
- Do a weekly review: clean up, check priorities, and plan key tasks for the coming week to avoid overload.
    
- Use simple rules such as “touch it once, decide it once” to reduce mental clutter and decision fatigue.
