- The “Victory Hour”: use the first 60 minutes of the day (ideally 5–6 a.m.) for intentional activities before normal work begins.
    
- Structure it as **20 minutes move, 20 minutes reflect, 20 minutes grow**:
    
    - Move: light exercise or stretching to boost energy and mood.
        
    - Reflect: journaling, gratitude, visualization, or planning your day.
        
    - Grow: focused learning (reading, podcasts, skill work) without distraction.
        
- Use routines and habits (consistent sleep, no phone upon waking) to make waking early sustainable.
    
- Link the early routine to your “big why” (purpose, values, long‑term goals) to increase motivation and discipline.
