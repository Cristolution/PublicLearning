# Quick Glossary 📖

*All key terms in one place!*

---

## A

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **AI** | Artificial Intelligence - machines that think/learn |
| **API** | Application Programming Interface - how apps connect |
| **Agent** | AI that can think, plan, and act on its own |
| **AutoGen** | Microsoft's framework for multi-agent systems |

---

## B

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **Batch Processing** | Running the same task on many items at once |
| **BLEU** | Metric for measuring translation quality |
| **Bias** | When AI has unfair preferences |

---

## C

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **Chain of Thought (CoT)** | Prompting technique to make AI think step-by-step |
| **Claude** | AI assistant from Anthropic |
| **CLI** | Command Line Interface - text-based interface |
| **Context Window** | How much AI can see/remember in one go |
| **CrewAI** | Framework for building AI agent teams |
| **Caching** | Saving responses to reuse later |

---

## D

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **DALL-E** | OpenAI's image generation tool |
| **DAG** | Directed Acyclic Graph - workflow structure |

---

## E

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **Embedding** | Converting text to numbers that represent meaning |
| **Evaluation** | Testing if AI is working well |

---

## F

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **Fine-tuning** | Training a model further on specific data |
| **Few-shot** | Giving AI examples in the prompt |
| **Function Calling** | AI using external tools/functions |
| **Framework** | A toolkit for building with AI |

---

## G

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **Gemini** | Google's AI model |
| **GPT** | Generative Pre-trained Transformer - OpenAI's models |
| **Guardrails** | Safety rules for AI behavior |

---

## H

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **Hallucination** | When AI makes things up that aren't true |
| **Human-in-the-Loop** | Keeping humans involved in AI processes |

---

## I

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **Inference** | Using a trained AI model |
| **Integration** | Connecting two services to work together |
| **Indexing** | Organizing data for fast search |

---

## L

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **LangChain** | Popular framework for building AI apps |
| **LangGraph** | LangChain's tool for agent workflows |
| **Latency** | How fast AI responds |
| **LLM** | Large Language Model - big AI that reads/writes text |
| **LoRA** | Low-Rank Adaptation - efficient fine-tuning |

---

## M

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **MCP** | Model Context Protocol - standard for AI tool connections |
| **Memory** | What AI remembers |
| **Model** | The AI program/version |
| **Multimodal** | AI that handles multiple types (text, images, audio) |

---

## N

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **NLP** | Natural Language Processing - AI understanding language |
| **No-Code** | Building without programming |

---

## O

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **Ollama** | Tool to run AI models on your computer for free |
| **OpenAI** | Company behind GPT, DALL-E, ChatGPT |
| **Orchestration** | Coordinating multiple AI agents |

---

## P

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **Parameters** | Internal settings in an AI model |
| **Plugin** | Extension that adds features to AI |
| **Pre-training** | Initial training of an AI model |
| **Prompt** | What you say to AI |
| **Prompt Engineering** | Writing good prompts |
| **Prompt Injection** | Tricking AI into ignoring rules |

---

## Q

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **Quantization** | Making models smaller/faster |

---

## R

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **RAG** | Retrieval Augmented Generation - giving AI reference material |
| **Red Teaming** | Testing AI to find weaknesses |
| **RLHF** | Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback |
| **Routing** | Directing requests to appropriate AI |

---

## S

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **SDK** | Software Development Kit - tools for building |
| **Semantic Search** | Searching by meaning, not just keywords |
| **Skill** | What an AI agent can do |
| **Streaming** | Getting AI response as it's generated |
| **System Prompt** | Hidden instructions that set AI behavior |

---

## T

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **Temperature** | How creative vs. predictable AI is |
| **Token** | Smallest unit of text AI processes |
| **Tool** | Something AI can use to do things |
| **Training** | Teaching AI from data |
| **Trigger** | What starts an automated workflow |

---

## V

| Term | Meaning |

| **Vector Database** | Database optimized for similarity search |

## W

| Term | Meaning |

## Z
